Origin · The Lawsuit

One letter,one pivot, one name.

We were imsu. Two friends, one wok, one extremely confident plan to open a noodle & sushi bar.

Then one ordinary lunchtime, a manager from a certain green-and-white rival noodle chain walked in. We thought it was a customer. It was a problem. A few weeks later: a lawsuit. Change the name, they said. Or else.

So we sat down, looked at each other, looked at our woks, and said — fine. That's how we became SUME. Pronounced like sue-me. The name is also the apology.

"Same kitchen. Same recipes. Same people. Different name. Better story."

Made today. Eaten today. Forgotten tomorrow — until you crave it again.

— The Founders
